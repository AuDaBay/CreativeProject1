Determination

How have I prepared myself to be a physician?

I worked as Home Mentor at a residential treatment center to gain the skills of not just how to help people follow a treatment plan, or to take meds, but to understand the importance of relationships in healing, and being healed.

I have joined multiple research groups and gained a variety of skills from my experiene. From how to give injections, to analyzing data. Most recently I have been participating in a machine learning project which has pushed me to my limits, and taught me how to be engaged in pro-active learning of topics that were beyond my reach.

I've shadowed a few doctors, as well as taken a course named Surgical Decision by Dr. Brian Gill. He discoursed on his experience from both medical student to Lead Trauma Surgeon and the choices and situations that were involved along the course of his experienec. It was eye opening, but exciting. As it was confirmed in my heart that this was the directoin I was suppose to go.
