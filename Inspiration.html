Inspiration

Why Did I Choose Medicine?

I've always been drawn to the idea of helping people, and there would be nothing more satisfying each day than to provide relief to someone who is suffering.

I've always loved solving puzzles, and every procedure, every diagnosis is a puzzle to be solved

I love the human body. They way it works, the miracle of its function, and all that it can do. But for some it is a limitation. I want to help others so that their imagination can be the limiter, and not their circumstance.
