A Little Bit About Me

Volleyball was my first love. I am crazy about the sport, and play it every chance that I get. I love the strategy, the mental game, and physical prowess involved. I can play for hours on end, and relish the days that I get to!

Near the end of my undergraduate, I discovered a new passion of mine for coaching. I have been coaching at High Altitude as a power tumbling and trampoline coach for about 2 years now. It has become my second home, and become near and dear to my heart. I honestly can't imagine life without tumbling anymore. I love the athletes that I work with and being able to see them improve and compete.

Of course the queen of all my passions is my best friend and wife Hannah. She means the world to me, and I could lose any one of my passions except for her. Our little family means the world to me, and they always come first in my heart.
